I am Associate Professor of Philosophy at Mount Holyoke College. I work mostly in ethics and epistemology, but I also have interests in free will, moral responsibly, social, political, and feminist philosophy. In general, I’m interested in how rational belief and action works for limited, fallible creatures like us.

People call me ‘Katia’. You can too.
